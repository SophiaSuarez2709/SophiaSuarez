Movie

2008

Wild Child

Growth Self-Discovery Friendship

A journey of growth that I deeply connect with. At first, it shows a carefree and rebellious side, but as the story unfolds, it becomes clear that real strength comes from discipline, self-awareness, and meaningful relationships.

I love how the film balances fun and humor with important life lessons about accountability, friendship, and finding your true identity. It reminds me that even when we make mistakes, we can still change, grow, and become better versions of ourselves.

Manhua & Anime

The thrilling push-and-pull dynamic where every interaction feels like a clever mind game. I love how it turns even the smallest moment into a battle of pride and strategy, building up romance but intentionally delaying it—creating tension that keeps me hooked.

The intense thrill of every high-stakes gamble and psychological twist. The unpredictability, bold personalities, and constant tension make every episode feel exciting. That adrenaline-like excitement through unique storytelling is what I live for.
